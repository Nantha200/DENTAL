Root Canal Treatment (RCT) is a procedure used to save a severely infected or damaged tooth. Instead of extracting the tooth, the infected pulp is removed, the canal is cleaned, disinfected, and sealed ‚Äî helping you keep your natural smile intact.

üß∞ Root Canal Procedure Steps:

  • Diagnosis with digital X-rays and consultation.
  • Removal of infected pulp and cleaning of root canals.
  • Disinfection and shaping of the canals.
  • Filling the canals with biocompatible material (gutta-percha).
  • Sealing with a dental crown for strength and protection.

Our expert endodontists use advanced technology to ensure the procedure is painless, precise, and successful in a single or few visits.
